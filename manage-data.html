Data Management and Deletion for [Your App Name]

Our Commitment to Your Privacy: At [Your App Name], we believe in transparency. Our application provides translation services by using a webview to access third-party platforms. We do not create user accounts or store your translation history on our own servers. All data you submit is handled directly by the service you choose.

To manage or delete your data, you must go to the respective service's data management portal. Below are instructions for each service available in our app.

Google Translate

Your Google Translate history may be saved to your Google Account if your "Web & App Activity" setting is enabled. You can view and delete this data by following these steps:

  1. Go to the Google My Activity page for your account:
    https://myactivity.google.com/myactivity
  2. In the search bar at the top of the page, type "Translate" to filter for your translation history.
  3. You can delete individual items by clicking the three-dot menu next to an item and selecting "Delete".
  4. To delete history for a specific period, use the "Delete activity by" option in the left-hand menu.

Microsoft Bing Translate

Your translation activity on Bing is managed through the Microsoft Privacy Dashboard. Follow these steps to clear your history:

  1. Visit the Microsoft Privacy Dashboard and log in to your Microsoft Account:
    https://account.microsoft.com/privacy/activity-history
  2. On the "Activity history" page, use the "Filter by data type" option on the right.
  3. Select "Voice" or look for search entries related to your translations. Microsoft may bundle translation history under Search or Voice.
  4. Click "Clear activity" for an item or "Clear all activity" for a specific data type to delete your history.

Anuvaad Bhashini

Bhashini is an initiative by the Government of India. As a public service, its data handling may differ. Data submitted to Bhashini is often used to improve the accuracy of AI models for Indian languages.

While Bhashini does not currently offer a public-facing dashboard for individual data deletion like Google or Microsoft, they are bound by the data and privacy policies of the Government of India.

  • You can review the platform's policies and terms of service on their official portal: https://bhashini.gov.in/
  • For specific data-related queries, you would typically need to contact the platform operators directly through their "Contact Us" or support channels provided on their website.
